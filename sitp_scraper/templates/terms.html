Privacy Policy

We develop SITP Bot to send you the information about SITP (Sistema Integrado de Transporte Público de Bogotá) routes and bus stations directly to your Facebook Messenger or Telegram chat. We know that you care about how your data is used, and we take your privacy very seriously. By using SITP Bot, you are accepting the practices outlined in this policy.

We don’t store any user specific data except the public information. This is the only information we keep.

Our servers also collect log information used to identify bugs in our code. The information includes data sent to and from Facebook Messenger and Telegram. The old data is automatically deleted once log is full.

Since we don’t know your email or phone number, we communicate with you only through your Facebook Messenger or Telegram account. But we don’t initiate discussion ourselves – we always wait for you to start. We do not sell your information to anyone. We don’t encrypt the data stored on our servers, but still it is safe.
